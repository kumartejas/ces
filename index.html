SERIES OF ACTIVITIES

GRAND SYMPOSIUM

             Global Youth Model United Nations will have the International Grand
             Symposium in the opening ceremony and delegates will be introduced
             to established and renowned leaders who will address them about the
             event theme and share their valuable ideas for inspiration and future
             implementation





Committee Sessions

             These sessions will include delegate recreating the UN assemblies and
             will be talking on the behalf of their assigned countries . Topics will be
             from a very broad spectrum and will enable and motivate the participants
             to seek out information and present in a professional and diplomatic manner.




Diplomatic DInner

             This formal event at Global Youth Model United Nations 2020 will be specially
             organized so that the diverse pool of delegates can sit together and talk and
             interact with each other on their topics of interest. Not only that but it will also
             feature ambassadors and diplomats from different international organizations
             and Government agencies, and it will be a perfect opportunity to get to know
             their work and find counsel from their ideas.


Cultural Night

             Making full use of the perfect opportunity of having young dreamers
             from different parts of the world, GYMUN will also host a cultural
             night and delegates will showcase their traditional dresses and appealing
             performances.





Kuala Lumpur City Tour

              The heavenly city of Kuala Lumpur has many sights that deserve to be
             be seen and appreciated and for that matter, GYMUN 2020 also features
             a tour of the city so that participants can experience the lifestyle and the
             beautiful culture.
